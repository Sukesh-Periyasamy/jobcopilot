SMART SEARCH
============
  Multi-keyword tokenization (Abbott Bangalore Research Associate)
  Synonym expansion (biomedical → medical technology, medical device, healthcare, diagnostics)
  Collection boosting (+12 for domain match)
  Weighted scoring: title(10) > company(8) > collection(12) > location(4) > description(3)
  Faceted filters (auto-generated companies, locations, collections)
  Score breakdown (explainable ranking)
  Suggestions on empty results
